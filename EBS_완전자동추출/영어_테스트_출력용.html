Review by Jennifer Miller, October 5, 2018
Rating ★★★★☆
The hotel is very small. It has only 15 bedrooms. But it has the perfect location. It’s right in the city center, so everything is within walking distance. First, there is a large department store near the hotel. ⓐ The department store is on the corner of State Street and First Avenue. ⓑ There is also a convenience store just across from the hotel. And if you need money, ⓒ there is a bank between the hotel and the department store. And if you want to relax, ⓓ go to the park behind the hotel. It’s beautiful!
